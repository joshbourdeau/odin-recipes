Ancient Wisdom Waffles

Description

A waffle is a leavened batter or dough that's cooked between two hot plates to create a characteristic shape, size, and surface. Waffles are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, and are often golden brown in color.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Ancient Scrolls
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Preheat your waffle iron
  2. Whisk the dry ingredients together.
